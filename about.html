What is this?

Effective Hallucinations is a place where I publish conversations I have with AI — mostly Claude — about the nonprofit sector, philanthropy, and related ideas. Some are exploratory, some are practical, some are just interesting.

Why?

I used to blog. I miss sharing things I'm thinking about online. AI has become a genuine thinking partner for me in my work, and some of those conversations feel worth sharing — either because they're useful or because they surface something worth arguing with.

The name is a nod to the AI tendency to confidently generate plausible nonsense, and to the effective altruism movement that has shaped so much of contemporary philanthropy. Both feel relevant.
